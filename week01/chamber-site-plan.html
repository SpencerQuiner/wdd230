Overview

Site Purpose

To promote the business interests of chamber of commerce members and the Arrecife, Lanzarote area, attracting additional business, tourism, and people. To provide an information resource on local businesses, and events.

Audience

Business owners, investors, and visitors to the Arrecife Lanzarote area.

Scenarios

What local businesses are sponsoring floats for this years Carnival Parades.

What current construction projects are ongoing in the city.

What are the population demographics of the area.
